⚡ Features

Everything a Clinic Needs

A complete clinical management system built with JavaFX — fast, offline-capable, and beautifully designed.

👥
Patient Management
Register, edit, and track patients with full profiles — personal info, blood type, emergency contacts, and complete medical history.
🩺
Doctor Registry
Manage your medical staff — specializations, license numbers, consultation fees, experience, and availability scheduling.
📅
Appointment Booking
Book, complete, or cancel appointments with real-time conflict detection. Filter by date, status, and doctor specialization.
📋
Health Records
Comprehensive health records per patient — diagnoses, prescriptions, vitals (BP, HR, temperature, weight), and treatment notes.
🔊
Voice Assistant
Hardware speaker integration reads patient info, appointment details, and system alerts aloud — hands-free operation for busy clinical environments.
🔔
Live Notifications
Real-time notification center — every action (registration, booking, completion, deletion) sends an instant alert to the notification screen.

🏥 About Us

Built for Real Clinical Environments

HealthSystem Pro was developed as a final-year academic project by a passionate team of computer science students who wanted to solve a real problem — making healthcare management accessible, modern, and efficient.

The system is built on Java and JavaFX with a MySQL backend, featuring a clean blue & white clinical UI, hardware speaker TTS integration, and a fully reactive notification system.

🎯
Our Mission
Make clinical management affordable and accessible for small clinics and hospitals.
🛡
Our Values
Patient privacy, data security, and clean UX are non-negotiable principles.
🚀
Our Vision
Grow into a full hospital management suite used across Central Africa.
💡
Tech Stack
Java 17, JavaFX 26, MySQL, JDBC, CSS, TTS via System.Speech.
